Discovering the Hidden Gem of Ipoh: A Day at Tasik Cermin

April 28, 2024 posted by Admin Nat

The journey to Tasik Cermin is an adventure in itself. After a short drive from Ipoh city center, I arrived at the entrance of a limestone quarry, which serves as the gateway to the lake. The path leading to the lake is not immediately obvious, adding an element of mystery and excitement to the trip. After a brief walk through a tunnel carved into the limestone, I emerged into a breathtaking scene.

The first sight of Tasik Cermin is nothing short of magical. The lake, nestled in a natural limestone cavern, is surrounded by towering cliffs that reflect perfectly in the calm, emerald-green waters. The mirror-like surface of the lake creates a stunning reflection of the surrounding limestone formations, giving the place its name - Mirror Lake.

Tips for Visitors

Timing: The best time to visit Tasik Cermin is in the early morning or late afternoon when the light is soft, and the reflections are at their best. Footwear: Wear comfortable shoes suitable for walking on uneven terrain, as the path to the lake can be a bit rough. Essentials: Bring water, snacks, and insect repellent to ensure a comfortable visit.
